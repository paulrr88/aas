2016 Exhibition

Our annual Exhibition was held in late July and enjoyed by our many visitors. The "Best in Show" award was chosen by well-known local artist, Cameron Weller.


Caryl Smith - our 2015 winner - was delighted to be taking back home the Arun Rose Bowl, awarded by Cameron Weller, as the best painting in the show.


"Midnight Roses" was the stunning winner of our "best in show" award

Raffle Winner

Our Club President and professional artist, Liz Seward, created a lovely painting for us to raffle and to help us support local charity, St Barnabas Hospice. Many visitors kindly bought tickets and the painting was won by Lesley Sumner.


Club Chairman, Mick Porter, presents Lesley Sumner with her painting.


Cameron congratulated the whole club on maintaining its high standards and specially commended ...

'Golden King' - Ann McGarry
'Blue Iris' - Judith Armstrong
'A Helping Hand' - Margaret Cairns
'The Welsh Coast' - Liz Ford
'We Like It Here' - Val Hedger
